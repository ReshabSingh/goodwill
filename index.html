Our Process

Five clear steps from assignment to submission

Our field team follows a structured process to ensure accurate, timely, and confidential verification reporting for every assignment.

  1. 01

    Assignment Received

    We receive the verification assignment along with applicant details from the bank or NBFC.

  2. 02

    Field Investigation

    Our field executive plans and conducts the on-ground investigation as per the assignment requirements.

  3. 03

    Physical Verification

    A thorough physical verification of the address, property, employment, or business is carried out.

  4. 04

    Report Preparation

    Findings are documented in a detailed, standardized verification report.

  5. 05

    Submission to Client

    The completed report is submitted to the client within the committed turnaround time.

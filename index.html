FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Clear answers to common questions about eligibility, documents, timelines, rates, and repayment.

Eligibility depends on the loan type, income profile, credit history, employment or business stability, and lender-specific requirements.

Common documents include identity proof, address proof, income proof, bank statements, photographs, and loan-specific supporting documents.

Processing time varies by loan product, document readiness, verification, and partner approval timelines. Complete documents help avoid delays.

Interest rates are based on the selected loan, borrower profile, credit assessment, loan amount, tenure, and lending partner terms.

Repayment is usually handled through monthly instalments. Tenure, EMI amount, prepayment terms, and charges depend on lender policy.
